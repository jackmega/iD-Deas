Making a Dungeon with Illustrator

Things You'll Need

  • Adobe Illustrator (or a similar program)

Here's What to do.

  1. Open Adobe Illustrator
  2. Open a new document
  3. Make a letter sized document (or A4 if you use that size)
  4. Change the document units to cm (centimeters)
  5. Make a grid that is 30cm by 30cm with 30 dividers
  6. Use the rectangular tool to make the rooms and hallways
  7. For doorways use small rectangles to signify doors
  8. Use different symbols to signify each different thing you may need (stairs, statues, alters, tables, chairs, etc.)
  9. Once you have finished with rooms and hallways and a bit of decoration, lable each room with either numbers or names (do this on a seperate layer if you plan on handing out a version of this map to your players) (if you use numbers make sure you know what each number references to)
